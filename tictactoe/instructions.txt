This is a TicTacToe game, where you can choose different dimensions for the board

The players can decide on the board size, and compete against each other in a TicTacToe turnument.
Which lasts as many times as the players decide.
The final game score will apear on the screen when the players finish their game.

how to play:
each player will place a symbol- X/O on their turn by pressing the number of the block they want to place their symbol in.

example of a 3X3 game board:

1 | 2 | 3
__________
4 | 5 | 6
__________
7 | 8 | 9


how to run the game:

cd tictactoe
javac TicTacToeP\*.java 
java TicTacToeP.Main
